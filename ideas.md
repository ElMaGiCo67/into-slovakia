# INTO Slovakia – Design Brainstorm

## Context
INTO Slovakia s.r.o. is a B2B/B2C toner buyback and sales company operating in Bratislava and Trnava. Services: buying empty cartridges, selling original toners (24+ brands), printer repair, renovated toners. HP Partner.

## Idea A – Industrial Precision (probability: 0.07)

**Design Movement:** Bauhaus-inspired industrial minimalism
**Core Principles:**
- Grid-based asymmetry with heavy typographic hierarchy
- Monochromatic base with a single vivid accent (electric blue)
- Data-forward layout: prices, specs, and service tiers as visual anchors
- Ink/print metaphor: halftone textures, dot patterns, press-mark details

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0F1117) base, warm white (#F5F3EE) text, electric cyan-blue (#0066FF) accent. Evokes precision machinery and print industry authority.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column split on hero – left: oversized brand mark + tagline, right: action cards. Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and white.

**Signature Elements:**
- Halftone dot-grid background texture on hero
- Thick left-border accent lines on section headers
- Monospaced font for prices/codes

**Interaction Philosophy:** Crisp, instant transitions. Hover states reveal underlines and color fills. No decorative animations – only functional feedback.

**Animation:** Entrance: staggered fade-up on scroll. CTA buttons: fill-from-left on hover. No looping animations.

**Typography System:** Display: "Space Grotesk" (bold 700-900). Body: "IBM Plex Sans" (400/500). Prices/codes: "JetBrains Mono".

## Idea B – Clean Slovak Corporate (probability: 0.08)

**Design Movement:** Scandinavian-influenced corporate clarity
**Core Principles:**
- Generous whitespace as the primary design tool
- Warm off-white backgrounds with charcoal typography
- Trust signals (HP Partner badge, 24-month warranty) as visual anchors
- Structured card-based layout for services

**Color Philosophy:** Off-white (#FAFAF8) background, deep charcoal (#1C1C1E) text, forest green (#1A6B3C) primary accent. Green signals sustainability, reliability, and the recycling/buyback aspect.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with centered content, then alternating left/right content blocks. Service cards in a 3-column grid. Sticky top navigation.

**Signature Elements:**
- Leaf/recycling motif subtly integrated into brand mark
- Soft card shadows with rounded corners
- Progress-bar style "how it works" flow

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, gentle. Hover lifts cards. Scroll-triggered section reveals. Warm and approachable.

**Animation:** Fade-in-up on scroll with 0.1s stagger. Card hover: translateY(-4px) + shadow deepening. Nav: blur backdrop on scroll.

**Typography System:** Display: "Playfair Display" (700). Body: "Source Sans 3" (400/600). Accent labels: "DM Mono".

## Idea C – Dark Tech Authority (probability: 0.06)

**Design Movement:** Dark-mode industrial tech / B2B SaaS aesthetic
**Core Principles:**
- Dark slate base with sharp contrast elements
- Orange/amber accent for urgency and action (buyback CTA)
- Dense information architecture – professional buyers scan fast
- Structural dividers using diagonal clip-paths

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy-slate (#0D1B2A) base, crisp white text, amber (#F59E0B) accent for CTAs and highlights. Conveys authority, speed, and technical expertise.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed dark hero with floating stat cards. Diagonal section dividers. Two-column service split. Brand logo grid with hover reveals.

**Signature Elements:**
- Floating metric cards (e.g., "24 brands", "24h service")
- Diagonal clip-path section transitions
- Amber glow on primary CTA buttons

**Interaction Philosophy:** Fast, decisive. Hover states are immediate. Amber glow pulse on primary CTA. Parallax on hero background.

**Animation:** Hero: subtle parallax scroll. Stats: count-up on viewport entry. Section transitions: diagonal wipe. Button: amber glow pulse on hover.

**Typography System:** Display: "Syne" (800). Body: "Outfit" (400/500). Data: "Roboto Mono".

## Selected Approach: **Idea C – Dark Tech Authority**

Rationale: INTO Slovakia operates in a B2B-heavy environment. Dark tech aesthetic signals professionalism and authority to corporate buyers. The amber accent creates urgency for the buyback CTA (core revenue driver). Dense information layout suits buyers who need to scan quickly. Diagonal section transitions add visual energy without being frivolous.
